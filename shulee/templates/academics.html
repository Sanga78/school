Academics

Curriculum

Our academic programs are designed to offer a comprehensive education that prepares students for future success. We offer a broad range of subjects, including Mathematics, Science, Humanities, Languages, and the Arts.

  • Primary Education (Grades 1-5)
  • Secondary Education (Grades 6-12)
  • Advanced Placement Courses
  • Extracurricular Programs

Departments

Mathematics Department

Our Mathematics department focuses on developing critical thinking and problem-solving skills through courses ranging from basic arithmetic to advanced calculus.

Science Department

Our Science department offers courses in Physics, Chemistry, and Biology, with an emphasis on hands-on learning and research opportunities.

Humanities Department

The Humanities department encourages students to explore history, geography, and literature, fostering a deep understanding of the world around them.

Arts Department

Our Arts department nurtures creativity through courses in music, visual arts, and performing arts, allowing students to express themselves artistically.
