Our Academic Approach

Balancing foundational learning with creative engagement

At AIC LABORET GIRLS BOARDING PRIMARY, we believe in providing a holistic education that lays a strong foundation for future learning. Our approach emphasizes:

  • Interactive and engaging teaching methods
  • Individualized attention to meet diverse learning needs
  • Use of educational resources and technology
  • Development of critical thinking and problem-solving skills
  • Integration of cultural and moral values

Grade Level Programs

Tailored education for every stage of development

ECDE (Play Group, PP1, PP2): Early Childhood Development

Our ECDE program focuses on holistic development through play-based learning and early literacy skills. Key features include:

  • Play-based learning activities
  • Early literacy and numeracy skills development
  • Creative arts and music
  • Social and emotional development
  • Physical development and outdoor play
ECDE Details

Primary (Grades 1-6): Foundational Learning

Our primary program builds on ECDE foundations with a focus on core academic subjects and critical thinking. Key features include:

  • Comprehensive literacy and numeracy skills
  • Science and environmental studies
  • Social studies and cultural awareness
  • Language studies and communication skills
  • Extracurricular activities and sports
Primary Details

Junior Secondary (Grades 7-9): Transition to Secondary Education

Our junior secondary program prepares students for higher education with a focus on in-depth subject knowledge and practical skills. Key features include:

  • Advanced subject-specific learning
  • Introduction to technology and computer literacy
  • Practical skills and project-based learning
  • Career guidance and exploration
  • Leadership and personal development
