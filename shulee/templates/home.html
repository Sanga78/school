Principal's Message

Building Leaders,
One Girl at a Time

On behalf of the dedicated staff, county education officers, and the wider AIC family, I welcome you warmly to AIC Laboret Girls Boarding Primary School — a place where every girl is seen, valued, and empowered.

Our mission is to provide a safe, nurturing, and academically stimulating environment rooted in Christian values and the Africa Inland Church's rich heritage of servant leadership. We believe education is the most powerful tool for transformation.

Under the Competency-Based Curriculum, our teachers facilitate deep, experiential learning that prepares our pupils for the world beyond the classroom. Every girl who walks through our gates leaves with confidence, character, and capability.

CBC Curriculum Implementation

AIC Laboret has fully embraced Kenya's Competency-Based Curriculum, transitioning from rote memorization to skills-driven, experiential learning pathways that prepare every girl for Junior Secondary and beyond.

  • All teachers trained and CBC-certified by KICD
  • Formative assessments conducted each term
  • Learner portfolios maintained per MoE guidelines
  • Practical learning corners in all classrooms
  • Career & technical pathways introduced from Grade 4
✝

AIC Spiritual Framework

Sponsored by the Africa Inland Church, our school's spiritual identity is central to everything we do. Pastoral care, mentorship, and Christian fellowship form the backbone of life at Laboret.

  • Weekly chapel services & daily devotions
  • Girl mentorship programme led by AIC pastors
  • Counselling services by trained female counsellors
  • Christian Union active in all classes
  • Annual spiritual retreats for boarding pupils
