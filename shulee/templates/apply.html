📜 Parent / Guardian Declaration

I, the undersigned parent/guardian, hereby declare that:

1. All information provided in this application is true, accurate, and complete to the best of my knowledge.
2. I understand and agree to the school's code of conduct, fee payment schedule, and boarding regulations as outlined in the school prospectus.
3. I give my consent for the school to use my child's information for educational and administrative purposes, including NEMIS registration.
4. I acknowledge that submission of this application does not guarantee admission, and that the school reserves the right to admit or decline any applicant based on available places and assessment results.
5. I will ensure all school fees are paid before the commencement of each term.

  • Original Birth Certificate or Birth Notification

  • Last school report card (Term 1 or 2)
  • Leaving / Transfer Certificate from previous school
  • Parent/Guardian National ID copy
  • Immunization card (under 9 years)
  • Completed application form & registration fee
