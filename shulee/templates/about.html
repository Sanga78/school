Our Story

A legacy of educational excellence since 1985

Founded in 1985 by visionary educator Dr. Margaret Williamson, Greenwood High began as a small neighborhood school with just 120 students and 8 teachers. What started as a humble institution has grown into one of the region's most respected educational establishments.

Over the decades, we've remained committed to our founding principles of academic rigor, character development, and community engagement. Our beautiful 25-acre campus now serves over 1,500 students from diverse backgrounds, supported by a faculty of 120 dedicated professionals.

Greenwood High has consistently ranked among the top schools in the state for academic performance, with our graduates attending prestigious universities worldwide. Beyond academics, we take pride in our championship athletics programs, award-winning arts department, and innovative STEM initiatives.

Dr. Margaret Williamson, Founder (1925-2010)
